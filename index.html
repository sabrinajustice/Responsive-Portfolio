About Me

My name is Sabrina Justice. I have a BS in MIS (Management Information Systems) from CSU Sacramento, and possess CompTIA A+, Network+, and Microsoft MCSE certifications.

Building web applications allows me to combine my love of technology and my penchant for out-of-the-box problem solving, and draw from my fine arts background and design aesthetic to create applications that are attractive, intuitive, and provide a valuable service to the end user.
